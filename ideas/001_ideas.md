## Core Hook / Gameplay Loop

The main hook is **pushing for a better time score** — similar to Trackmania.

- Goal: achieve the perfect run
- Obstacles act as **checkpoints** that must be cleared in the correct order
- Hitting a checkpoint out of order results in a **penalty**
- Players will want to **repeat runs** to beat their personal best
- This creates a strong competitive element — you always have a target to beat

---

## Obstacles & Mechanics

### Checkpoints
- Must be cleared in the correct order
- Going out of order triggers a time penalty

### Speed Modifiers
- **Mud** — slows the dog down
- **Wet grass** — slows the dog down
- **Kids on the sides of the track** — dog runs slower

### Player Actions
- **Boost on Spacebar** — a speed boost to help shave off time

## Ghost / Competition System

- A **semi-transparent ghost dog** plays back the fastest run
- Shows exactly how the best-performing dog ran the course
- During gameplay, you always know who you need to beat
- This adds a strong competitive element — you want to beat the ghost
